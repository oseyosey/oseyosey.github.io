About

Hi, I’m Junjie Oscar Yin#

I conduct research in natural language processing and machine learning.

I am an incoming PhD student at UW NLP, advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi.

Currently, I am fortunate to be collaborating with Sasha Rush.

In my undergraduate years at Johns Hopkins, I am grateful to be advised by Alan Yuille and supported by the Westgate Scholarship. During my studies, I spent a year at University of Oxford, where I had the privilege of being mentored by Michael Wooldridge.
